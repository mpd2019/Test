the reasons

A number of health conditions can cause or worsen tinnitus. In many cases an exact cause has never been found A common cause of tinnitus is damage to the capillary nerve cells in the inner ear. Small and thin hairs move in your inner ear according to the sound waves it brings. This causes the cells to release an electrical signal through the auditory nerve from your ear to your brain. Your brain interprets these signals as sound. If the capillaries inside the inner ear are bent or broken, they can "leak" random electrical impulses into your brain, causing tinnitus Other causes of tinnitus include other ear problems, chronic health conditions, injuries or conditions affecting the nerves in your ear or the hearing center of your brain.

Common causes of tinnitus

In many people, tinnitus occurs due to one of these conditions Age-related hearing loss For many people, hearing worsens with age, and it usually begins around the age of sixty. Hearing loss can cause tinnitus. The medical term for this type of hearing loss is aging Exposure to loud noise Loud noises, such as from heavy equipment, chain saws and firearms, are common sources of noise-related hearing loss. Portable music devices, such as iPod and MP3 audio players, can cause noise noise hearing if played out loud for long periods of time. Tinnitus caused by short-term exposure, such as attending a loud concert, usually disappears, but short-term and long-term exposure to loud sound can cause permanent damage Earwax blockage Earwax protects the ear canal by trapping dirt and slowing the growth of bacteria. treatment

Unfortunately, there is no final treatment for tinnitus except for minor cases and through the treatment of etiology For example if the cause of tinnitus is earwax, you can treat by going to the doctor and cleaning the wax by suction using a special device or expelling it with warm water If you have an ear infection, your doctor will prescribe medications to treat it, and the tinnitus will disappear Surgery may be necessary in rare cases of a tumor, cyst, or hardening of the ear (which is a store of calcium on the bone of the ear) or patching of the eardrum If the tinnitus is due to the temporomandibular joint syndrome, a herniated disc that connects the skull to the lower jaw directly in front of the ear on the side of the face. Medicines to treat tinnitus For some, treatment with low doses of anti-anxiety medications, such as valium or antidepressants, such as: elafil, helps in reducing tinnitus, as well as using steroid in the middle ear with anti-anxiety medication. Retriever technique for tinnitus It is through which proportionality is achieved with hearing the same sounds, by accustoming the brain to hearing the same sounds heard by the injured person. There are also other ways to relieve or treat some conditions, such as using Chinese needles or using some nutritional supplement, such as the ginkgo plant But the best remedy for tinnitus is to cope with the disease by forgetting it, lacking stress and anxiety It is not dangerous, it is only annoying and may disappear in some cases without warning.
